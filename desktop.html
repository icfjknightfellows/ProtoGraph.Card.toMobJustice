April 15, 2017
Pehlu Khan (55) and others
Location
Alwar, Rajasthan (BJP ruled)
Names of the victims
Pehlu Khan (55), Azmat (22), Irshad, Aarif and Rafiq
What were the victims doing?
Muslim men farmers were legally transporting two milch cows to Haryana in a pickup truck.
What was the mob doing?
Hindu men gau rakshaks saw the victims's pick-up truck on the highway and assumed that they were transporting the bovines illegally.
Was it illegal?
The mob broke the law.
What happened?
The Gau Rakshaks intercepted the pick-up and proceeded to beat up the men. Pehlu Khan succumbs to injuries.

4 victims were injured and 1 victims were left dead.
